Annual Science & Innovation Exhibition 2024

Kwame Nkrumah University proudly presents the Annual Science & Innovation Exhibition, showcasing the remarkable achievements of our students in scientific research and technological innovation. This event brings together the brightest minds from across Zambia to demonstrate their solutions to real-world challenges.

The exhibition features projects from all four schools of the university, highlighting interdisciplinary collaboration and cutting-edge research. From sustainable agriculture solutions to digital innovation and healthcare advancements, witness the future of Zambian science and technology.

Event Highlights

  • Interactive presentations of student research projects
  • Keynote address by renowned Zambian scientist Dr. Chanda Mwape
  • Innovation competition with prizes for outstanding projects
  • Live demonstrations by School of Natural Sciences faculty
  • Networking session with industry professionals and researchers
